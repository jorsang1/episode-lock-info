Episode Lock is a browser extension that keeps track of where you are in a series and prevents accidental spoilers while browsing streaming platforms.

Get the Extension

Features

📍

Track Your Progress

Automatically saves the last episode you watched so you always know where you left off.

🚫

Spoiler Protection

Hides episode titles and thumbnails beyond your current progress to keep the story fresh.

📺

Multi-Platform Support

Works seamlessly across popular streaming platforms right in your browser.

🔒

Privacy First

All data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is ever sent to any server.

How It Works

  1. 1

    Install the Extension

    Add Episode Lock to your browser in one click from the Web Store or Add-ons page.

  2. 2

    Browse Your Streaming Platform

    Navigate to the series you are watching. Episode Lock activates automatically.

  3. 3

    Set Your Current Episode

    Use the extension popup to confirm which episode you are on. That's it!

  4. 4

    Enjoy Spoiler-Free Browsing

    Episodes beyond your locked position are automatically hidden as you browse.
