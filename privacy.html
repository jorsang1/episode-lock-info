Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

Thank you for using Episode Lock. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

1. Data We Collect

Episode Lock does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The extension operates entirely within your browser and stores all information locally using your browser's built-in storage API.

2. What Is Stored Locally

The following data may be stored locally in your browser:

  • The name of a series and the episode number you have locked as your current progress.
  • User preferences (e.g., whether spoiler protection is enabled for a given platform).

This data never leaves your device and is only accessible to the Episode Lock extension itself.

3. Permissions

Episode Lock requests only the permissions it needs to function:

  • storage – to save your episode progress and settings locally.
  • activeTab / host permissions for streaming platforms – to detect the current series page and apply spoiler protection.

4. Third-Party Services

Episode Lock does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is sent to any external server by this extension.

5. Children's Privacy

Episode Lock does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue in our GitHub repository.
