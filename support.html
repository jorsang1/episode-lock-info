Frequently Asked Questions

  • Click the Episode Lock icon in your browser's toolbar to open the popup. Navigate to the series you are watching, then use the popup to select or type your current episode number and click Lock.
  • Episode Lock currently supports the most popular streaming platforms. Support for additional platforms is being added over time. If you would like to request a new platform, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
  • Your data never leaves your device. All progress and settings are stored locally using your browser's built-in storage. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
  • Open the Episode Lock popup while on the series page and choose the new episode you are on. Click Lock to update your progress.
  • Open the Episode Lock popup, find the series you want to remove, and click the Remove or Unlock button next to it.
    1. Make sure you are using a supported browser (Chrome or Firefox).
    2. Try disabling and re-enabling the extension from your browser's extensions page.
    3. Check that the extension has the necessary permissions for the current website.
    4. If the problem persists, please report it on our GitHub Issues page.
