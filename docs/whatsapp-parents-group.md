# WhatsApp Parents Group message

Here is an informal message to use in the school WhatsApp group for parents, who might have kids between 3 and 8 or so,to share the news about EpisodeLock and invite them to check it out:

> Hi everyone! 👋 I wanted to share a new tool I’ve been working on called EpisodeLock. It’s designed to help parents manage their kids’ screen time on streaming platforms like Netflix and Disney+. 
For me it sucks when you tell them, ok, only 1 or 2 episodes, and then you need to go and make dinner or something, and they end up watching 4 or 5 episodes instead because you can't keep an eye on them all the time.
So, if you're using a laptop or a PC instead of a TV or a tablet, check out this chrome extension and see if it could help you too!
So far it’s been really helpful for me with Uxue. 😊
If you want to try it out, it is free, and I'd love to hear your feedback! You have all the info here: https://jorsang1.github.io/episode-lock-info/
